Background

I work across visual design, illustration, interactive projects, animation, and mixed media. My practice often begins with social observation or a personal question, then develops into posters, game systems, interface prototypes, moving images, or spatial experiences.

I am especially interested in how images can translate invisible emotions and complex issues into forms that are easier to approach, discuss, and remember.
